Intsonic offers application specific sensor devices design and fabrication, addressing a number of areas, including metrology (gas and liquid-coupled), non-destructive testing, proximity and level sensing. We also collaborate with other companies to provide full ultrasonic solutions.

Specifically, the company activities include: consultancy to develop proof of concept setups, development of complete systems/solutions, prototype manufacture, and low volume production. At the moment, our largest areas of expertise are air-coupled ultrasound and metrology.
