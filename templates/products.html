Intsonic "UUMM" board

A compact, low-power measurement platform for differential transit time or general purpose ultrasonic measurements.
Runs from 3.5-5V and is primarily intended for battery-powered applications. It can handle up transducers up to 1.5MHz with total insertion loss of up to -60db. The zero-flow drift is generally in the 25-150ps range for liquid-coupled applications, depending on the frequency and total inserion loss. The current version of the board supports multiplexing up to 16 transducers in pitch-catch and pulse-echo configurations. The data is transmitted via a serial connection (RS232/RS485 by default). Configuration is done using the same serial interface, with great flexibility. Powerful cross-correlation based algorithms are performed on the board with plenty of diagnostics data being generated. We are also working on a high-voltage version.
