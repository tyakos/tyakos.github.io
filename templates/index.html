At Intsonic limited we develop ultrasonic devices and solutions. You can find out more about our electronics solution on the Products page and follow the developments on the News page.

Our experience lies in the following areas, in the following order:

  • flow measurement (gas and liquid-coupled);
  • proximity and level sensing;
  • non-destructive testing (including non-contact).

Specifically, the company activities include:

  • transducer optimization (FEM and other tools) and prototyping;
  • development of PoCs;
  • development of complete systems/solutions;
  • low volume production.
