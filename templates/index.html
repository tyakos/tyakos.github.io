At Intsonic limited we develop ultrasonic devices and solutions. You can find out more about our electronics solution on the Products page and follow the developments on the News page.

We solve flow measurement challenges of all sizes. Whether it’s beer in a small tube, a 500mm water main, or wind speed at a weather station, we evaluate if ultrasonic technology is the right fit. If it is, we’ll design a custom solution for you.

Our experience lies in the following areas:

  • flow measurement (gas and liquid);
  • proximity and level sensing;
  • non-destructive testing (including non-contact).

Specifically, the company activities include:

  • R&D.
  • Licencing our ultrasonic measurement platform and adapting it to the specific application.
  • Transducer development, optimization (FEM and other tools) and prototyping.
  • Prototyping.
  • Low volume assembly/production.
