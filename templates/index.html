At Intsonic limited we develop ultrasonic devices. We are working on our independently developed water meter as well as provide consulting services. You can find out more about our DN50 inline water meter on the Products page and follow the development on the News page.

Our consulting services mostly include ultrasonic transducer design, optimization and fabrication, addressing a number of areas:

  • flow measurement (gas and liquid-coupled);
  • proximity and level sensing;
  • non-destructive testing (including non-contact).

Specifically, the company activities include:

  • transducer optimization (using PZFlex and other tools) and prototyping;
  • development of PoCs;
  • development of complete systems/solutions;
  • low volume production.

At the moment, our largest area of expertise is flow measurement.
