Intsonic offers application specific sensor devices design and fabrication, addressing a number of areas:

  • metrology (gas and liquid-coupled);
  • proximity and level sensing;
  • non-destructive testing (including non-contact).

Specifically, the company activities include:

  • consultancy to develop proof of concept setups;
  • development of complete systems/solutions;
  • prototype manufacture;
  • low volume production.

At the moment, our largest areas of expertise are air-coupled ultrasound and metrology.
We also collaborate with other companies to provide full ultrasonic solutions.
