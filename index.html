About us:

Intsonic offers application specific sensor devices design and fabrication, addressing a large number of areas, including (but not limited to) oil/gas, automotive, robotics and other industrial ultrasonic applications.

Vision:

The main objective is to create an innovative technology company for design/manufacture of ultrasonic devices and solutions. Our company strives to utilize advanced production methods to create application specific device design, while minimizing manufacturing costs to provide the growing market with opportunities to use UT in their various products/solutions, both existing and new.

Approach:

Intsonic aims to offer a strong knowledge base and continuous innovation. We are planning to adapt advance manufacturing methods from other areas of manufacturing to UT production, individual approach and flexibility to be able to expand and adjust to market needs. Specifically, the company activities include: consultancy to develop from proof of concept setups, provision of complete systems/solutions, prototype manufacture, and low volume production.

Also, our company is looking to rapidly expand into a turnkey ultrasonic solutions provider by collaborating with companies on the following areas: materials (ceramic, functional polymers/plastics etc.), electronics and software design/manufacture, and data analytics.
